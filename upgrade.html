This means you must be using a standards-compliant web browser to view this site. While 95% of our audience uses a standards-compliant browser, you appear not to be using one. We would like to help you improve your experience at Sybil Shane Studio as well as the rest of the Internet.

Click on one of the links at the left to upgrade your browser for free. To learn more about why you reached this page, read on.

 

Why have I reached this page?

The short answer is that everything ages and must eventually replaced. To have reached this page, the browser you are using must be at least 5 years old. On the internet, this is several lifetimes ago. Much like 8-tracks gave way to cassettes, which gave way to CDs, which will one day give way to something else, old browsers give way to new browsers. Since less than 5% of the visitors to this site use out-dated browsers, this site was designed to take advantage of all the new features that newer browsers could offer. During the course of the next year or so, we expect many, if not most, websites to begin redesigns to take advantage of these same features.

Fortunately for you, upgrading your browser does not cost anything: all browsers are available for FREE from the people who make them. To make this process just a little bit easier for you, we've had a look at what browser and operating system you're currently running and created a list of options for you to choose from. To improve your web-browsing experience, please click on a link to the left, download the browser, and install it.

If you'd like a more technical explanation of this, please visit The Web Standards Project's Browser upgrade campaign page.
