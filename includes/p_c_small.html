Small Framed Print — Calligraphy

This art print measures 9" x 9" square. It is matted in a silk-textured high quality mat under glass, and framed in a contemporary black wooden frame. (This is NOT an endangered hardwood!) The mat is hand signed with the name of the image. The square format is very popular. It presents beautifully when hung on the wall, or placed on a desk or nightstand.
