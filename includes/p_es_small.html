Small Framed Print — Essence of the Seasons

This art print measures 9" x 9" square. It is matted in a silk-textured high quality mat under glass, and framed in a contemporary black wooden frame. (This is not an endangered hardwood!) The image is digitally signed. The square format is very popular, and these prints look great hung together as a series of four in a square on the wall. They also present beautifully when placed on a desk or nightstand.
